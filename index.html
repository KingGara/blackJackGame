BlackJack Rules

Objective

The objective of the game is to beat the Dealer. This can be done by either receiving cards that total a sum of 21 (automatic win), OR by getting as close to 21 as possible, standing then allowing the Dealer to "BUST". Game begins with Player & Dealer both being dealt two "hole" cards. Both the Players cards are dealt face up, while only one of the Dealers cards is dealt face up. Player acts first & can either "STAND" or "HIT".


Actions

If Player choses to "HIT" they will recieve another card. All numeric cards 2-10 equal the given value, all face cards equal 10 & aces can either equal 1 or 11 according to what the Player wants. If at anypoint the sum of the cards dealt to Player exceeds 21, the hand is over & the dealer wins.

If Player is satisfied with the sum of the hole cards or the sum of all cards currently dealt, the Player can choose to "STAND". This means they forfeit thier ability to "HIT" & whatever sum they currently have is what they will "STAND" with against the Dealer.
